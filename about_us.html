About us

The VR In Space website is created by a team of 10 eighth-grade students from Weston Middle School and have been researching and designing solutions to solve loneliness and stress and space. Together, we came up with the idea of VR with smells. Our main goal is to help astronauts cope with stress and help them remember home. All ten of us are also part of a robotics team called The Quaker Oates and are competing in the 2018 First Lego League competition.
